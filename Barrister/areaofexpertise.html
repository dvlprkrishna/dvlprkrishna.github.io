Labour Laws

Labour law mediates the relationship between workers, companies, factories, trade unions and the government. Adv. Seema Chopda has over 20 years of experience with Labour Laws as she started her practice as a Labour Law specialist being the daughter of renowned Bombay High Court Judge and Leading Labour Law Practicioner in his time, Justice Rajan Kochar (Retd.). She represents a number of Employees, Labour Unions and Employers in various court in various matters. She has represented INTUC and ST Workers Union, among others. The following are the kinds of matters she specializes in:

  1. Termination
  2. Retrenchment
  3. Employees State Insurance (ESI)
  4. Employees Provident Fund
  5. Service Conditions
  6. Promotions
  7. Gratuity
  8. Pension
  9. Employees Compensation in case of accidents on duty
