Nikola Tesla was an Engineer, Physicist, Fururist known for designing the alternating-current (AC) electric system, which is still the predominant electrical system used across the world today.

He also created the "Tesla coil," which is still used in radio technology.

He has a minimum of 278 patents, although most of his inventions were never under patent protection.

Some of his inventions are Remote Control, X -rays, Radio, Electric Motor, Radar and many more.
