Power of Attorney to Collect Debts

To All to Whom These Presents Shall Come, I am Mr. Ronak Kadhi, aged 72 years residing at, Dosti Pinnacle, Unit No G1, Plot No E7, Road No 22, Wagle Industrial Estate, Thane West, Thane, Maharashtra 400604

Whereas in the course of my business, i have several amounts to recover from others and which were either advances or loans or an account payments or as deposits or arising out of sale and purchase of goods.

And Whereas due to my old age, i am unable to take the necessary action to recover the debts and I, therefore, propose to appoint some fir and proper person as my Attorney to do all acts and things in that behalf which he has agreed to do.

Now know you all and these presents witness that i, the said Mr. Ronak Kadhi do hereby appoint the sole attorney as my true and lawful attorney with full power and authority to do and execute all acts, deeds, and things as hereinafter mentioned.

The Attorney Deepak Mishra, aged 39 years residing at, C-49, G Block, Bandra Kurla Complex, Bandra East, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400051 shall be authorised to collect all of the payments due to me.
