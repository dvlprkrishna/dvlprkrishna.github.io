Frequently Asked Questions

You got question, we got answers.

  • Can i use the Free Themes on multiple sites?

    No, you cannot use free themes on multiple sites. You must buy multi domain license, to use theme on multiple sites. contact us for further questions.

  • Can i use the Premium themes on multiple sites?

    Yes, you are free to use our Premium themes on as many websites as you like. We do not place any restrictions on how many times you can download or use a theme, nor do we limit the number of domains that you can install our themes to.

  • Do i get Email Support?

    Yes, we provide Email support for premium themes and multi domain license.

  • Do i get Regular Updates?

    Yes, we provide regular updates for both Free and Premium themes.

  • Do you provide code documentation?

    Yes, we provide code documentation. our code are well commented, we provide separate user manuals as well.

  • What does Multi domain license means?

    Multi domain license, allows you to use free themes on multiple sites/domain. You will get code documentation, Email support, and our love.
