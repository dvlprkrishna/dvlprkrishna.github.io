About Us

Adv. Seema Chopda has 20 years of Experience.
Since 1995, when she received her Certificate of Practice, she has been at her mission of providing quality legal services at optimal service considerations to anyone who approaches her humble doorstep. Known for her integrity and ability to connect with her clients, colleagues, juniors and seniors alike, she is a gem to the legal industry as she brings a touch of humanity to the field. Daughter of Retired Bombay High Court Judge, Justice R. J. Kochar, her aim is to carry forward the legacy and be the beacon of hope in this field.

With humble beginnings in the field of Labour Law, she now practices in a wide array of areas like Consumer Law, Civil Litigation, Property Law, Family Law and others, with equal dedication to each.
