Nikola Tesla was an engineer known for designing the alternating-current (AC) electric system, which is still the predominant electrical system used across the world today.

He also created the "Tesla coil," which is still used in radio technology.

Tesla came to the United States in 1884 and briefly worked with Thomas Edison
